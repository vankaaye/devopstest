About MCA

With 50 teams and 1200 participants last year, the Melbourne Cricket Association is proud to bring another season of a well-organised, fun and professional cricket competition.

🏈

Games Every Saturday

Consistent weekly fixtures so you can plan your season from day one.

📍

Live Scored on PlayHQ

All games are live scored on PlayHQ for real-time updates and stats.

🎥

Live Streamed

Selected games live streamed via Frogbox on YouTube and the PlayCricket mobile app.

⚖

Professional Umpires

Professional umpires for every game, including umpires from Premier Cricket.

🏆

Separate Divisions

Separate divisions for club's 1st & 2nd XI teams to ensure fair competition.

🎉

Awards & Trophies

Huge trophies, medals and awards for winners, runners, best batsman, bowler, fielder, most sixes, and more.
